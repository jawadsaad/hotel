EAT & DRINK

WALL TO WALL DINING

From stunning buffet dining to comforting local food or shisha and fresh juice cocktails in The Garden to coffee on the couch, whatever your fancy, Al Sayedah Zeinab Hotel has a suitable spot. Its location close to the center of Damascus makes it a perfect destination for meetings, conferences, working lunches, weekend brunches, special dinners and bespoke private dining events.

Guests staying with us at the hotel enjoy a free daily breakfast buffet at Yamama Restaurant. Indulge in our wonderful selection of home-made pastries and sandwiches, cereals, eggs, cheese, labneh, fresh fruit and much more. Complimentary tea, coffee and juices available as well.

DINING & RECREATION

RELAX AT

THE LOUNGE

The perfect place to relax in an Oriental atmosphere, offering hot and cold beverages in addition to light snacks.

❮ ❯

GLORIOUS BREAKFAST BUFFETS AT

YAMAMA

Every morning, Yamama becomes a buzzing social hotspot with our fantastic breakfast buffet. Find everything from traditional egg breakfasts to continental pastries and cereals. For the remainder of the day, enjoy our selection of Oriental and International à la carte menus.

❮ ❯

IRANIAN CUISINE AT

SHEBSTAN

Syria's first Iranian restaurant distinguished by it's typical Iranian food, prepared by specialised Iranian chefs. Fresh Iranian bread baked live in front of you.

*Unfortunately, due to the ongoing conflict in Syria, Shebstan is currently serving the same menu as Yamama.

❮ ❯

TAKE IN THE SCENERY AT

THE GARDEN

Provides you with a very comfortable atmosphere and an outside relaxing evening in our garden on the Mezzanine floor, surrounded by beautiful, colourful flowers. Come along for lunch, dinner or just some light-snacking and enjoy a shisha pipe with your friends and family.

❮ ❯

ENJOY THE GAME AT

FRESH DELIGHTS

Come along with your friends and family to benefit from our outside tent. Enjoy a selection of saj, pizzas, burgers, shawarma, fresh juices and shisha. Catch the game on our flatscreen TVs or feel free to play tables, chess and cards.
