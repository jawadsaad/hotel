MEETINGS & EVENTS

For your special events, the hotel offers an array of state-of-the-art facilities and services. Our team will take care of your conferences, seminars, business meetings, dinners and weddings, assisting you with unmatched pampering and professionalism to guarantee you a memorable experience.

The hotel boasts a ballroom, two meeting rooms, two restaurants available for private hire and The Garden, meaning whatever event you have in mind, we've got a suitable setting to bring it to life.
